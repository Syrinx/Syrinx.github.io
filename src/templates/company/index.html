THE ORIGINS

Founded in 1998, Syrinx is based in Needham, Massachusetts and has been recognized on the Boston Business Journal's List of "Areas Largest IT Consulting Firms" and the "Inc. 500" lists. Syrinx has built a strong reputation developing solutions within the financial services, retail, mobile, internet, call center, real estate, ed tech, publishing, health care, and biosciences industries.

What does Syrinx mean?

People sometimes ask us, "What does Syrinx mean?" In classical mythology, Syrinx was a river nymph who was pursued by the Greek god Pan. To escape his advances, she ran to the river's edge and was transformed into hollow water reeds.The reed is the classic symbol of resilience in the face of change. And we channel the qualities of transformation and resilience into our approach to solving business problems with innovation and agility.

Andrew Gelina
