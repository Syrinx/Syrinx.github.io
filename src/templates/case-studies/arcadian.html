Acadian Asset Management

Acadian Asset Management is a global, quantitative, investment manager.

Syrinx works with Acadian Asset Management to design and develop applications to support their enterprise. We develop and maintain numerous core business applications leveraging C#, and MS SQL Server as we help the company advance toward its target architecture and development practices. We have been building out automation capabilities for benchmarking/performance profiling of services. We work with the Acadian engineering team to design and build out load testing environments in AWS as well as integrate this testing platform into CI/CD pipelines.
